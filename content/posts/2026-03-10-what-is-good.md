## Good in the last week

- [Mutiny After Midnight](https://www.mutinyaftermidnight.com/) - I know I 
mentioned it last time but it's just so good. Between this and Sound & Fury, he's
cornered the market on Rolling Stones-style '70s scuzz rock and I love that
- [Hardcover](https://hardcover.app/welcome). Used to be the only way I knew GoodReads
got updated by some intern at Amazon was when my bookmarklets broke. Now there's a
place in the same vein that seems like it will be a great community for at least a
bit if it gains traction. Already built myself a [userscript for finding books
at my local library](https://gist.github.com/tclancy/6d1f6955c0d788febac296280e87c353)
- _The Pitt_. I'm late to it and the 100th person to tell you, but still. Could be
about almost anything with the writers, acting talent and direction
