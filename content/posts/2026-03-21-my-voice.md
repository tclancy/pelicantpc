# Finding My Voice

For ... reasons, I fed Claude a bunch of my Internet posting history. In
retrospect, much of the worst of it

- Metafilter comments (not so bad)
- Reddit archive (not great)
- Twitter archive (very not great)

I wanted to see how well it could match my overworked, purple prose style.
Don't think I paved any new ground or made something useful outside of one
or two well-defined tasks, but the result was illuminating.
